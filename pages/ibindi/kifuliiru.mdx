# Kifuliiru

## Tumenye bingi ku ndeto yitu Kifuliiru. 

Indeto iliri muberangirwa Kifuliiru, imuganulwa ku Kihugo kye' Congo. Ino ndeto inabe iliri ya' bandu abamuberangirwa **Bafuliiru**.
Bano bandu batuziri mu Kihugo kye' **Congo** mu Territoire ye' Buvira (Uvira). 

Kino kibaja kiliri hano tumenye bingi ku yeno ndeto yitu, Kifuliiru; ndeto nyija, ndeto ngale.

Mwangamenya bingi ku Kifuliiru na mu Kifuliiru [hano](https://ibufuliiru.editorx.io/ibufuliiru/kifuliiru)
