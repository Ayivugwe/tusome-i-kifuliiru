# Menya gano maziina ku baandu na ku binamishwa

Umufuliiru agweeti ngisi kwo amudeta umwaana wo'muundu na ngisi kwo aliri mudeta ikyaana kye'kinamishwa.

Kiri na hano mw'itwe ly'amagaambo mugabona kwo kera twahushanisa gano magambo: Ku muundu tumudeta _Umwaana_ mu buniini na mu bwiingi tunadete _Abaana_. Ku binamishwa Umufuliiru amudeta _Ikyaana_ ku buniini na mu bwiingi, Umufuliiru amudeta _Ibyaana_. Tuloongage mu bwiingi ukushagirana na ngisi muundu kandi li ikinamishwa.
