# Ibikola byo' lugero luhamu bweneene

Soma ibindu bye'Kifuliiru ibikola ku lugero luhamuna bweneene. Ibindu ibikola hala bweneene.
Tuyiji bingi mu Kifuliiru na mu zindi ndeto. Yibyo byoshi ngisi byo tuyiji mu zindi ndeto tubilete na mu ndeto yitu Kifuliitu.
Tugakizi kiyiga no kuyigirizania bwija bwija. Tugamenya bingi.
