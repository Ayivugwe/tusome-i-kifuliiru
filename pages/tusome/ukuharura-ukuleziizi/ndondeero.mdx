# Ukuharura ukuleziizi

Ku kyanya kera twaharura amagambi, ibindu, ... mu Kifuliiru, haliri ahandu ho tumukira tunagwanane kwo kera twahekerwa, twayananirwa higulu ubwenge bwo'mwona wo'mundu haliri ho bugalukiri. Ku yikyo kyanya binahunage kwo tukolese igindi njira yo'kuharura, injira ileziizi, yo'lugero lwa higulu. Tukengere kwo ahalimbi kitu, kuliri kugwasanya tuhike ku ndaambo yo kuharura BINGI MU KIFULIIRU.

Hano tugabonaga bingi mu yibyo ibikolihanisiri mu kuharura.
