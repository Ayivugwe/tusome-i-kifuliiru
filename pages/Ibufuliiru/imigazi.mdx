# Imigazi ye'Bufuliiru

Imwitu Ibufuliiru tugweeti imigazi mingi. Ukuba Mufuliiru unabe utayiji imigazi ye'mwitu Ibufuliiru kuliri kuhomba bweneene. Byo bitumiri hano twasima tuyerekane 
imigazi yooshi tugweti Ibufuliiru.

Ibufuliiru yo mwitu. Tuhatuziri. Tuhayiji, tumuhasima, yo'mwitu. Tumenye bingi ku hano handu tumuberangira iwmitu, ikaya; ngisi munda amakondo gitu, ga ba data na ba mawe, bashokulu na ba mugaka galiri mazike.

Kino kibaja kiliri hano kitumenyese bingi ku mwitu, Ibufuliiru. Bo bugale bwitu, ko kalaga kitu.
