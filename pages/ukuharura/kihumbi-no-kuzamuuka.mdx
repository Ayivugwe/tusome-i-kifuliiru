# Kihumbi ukuzamuuka (1000-)

Harura kushagira ku kihumbi kugenderera.

Higulu ukuharura kutamumala, hano ho tukolera ku kuharura kushagira ku kihumbi ukugenderera, tukola tugahana naho injira yo'kulengamwohe ukuharura imiharuro mihamu mihamu.

## Kushaga ku kihumbi kuhisa ku bihumbi mweenda (1.000 - 9.000)

1000 = Kihumbi  
2000 = Bihumbi bibiri  
3000 = Bihumbi bishatu  
4000 = Bihumbi bina  
5000 = Bihumbi bitanu  
6000 = Bihumbi ndatu  
7000 = Bihumbi birinda  
8000 = Bihumbi munaana  
9000 = Bihumbi mweenda  
10.000 = Bihumbi ikumi

## Kushaga ku bihumbi makumi gabiri kuhisa ku bihumbi igana (10.000 - 100.000)

20.000 = Bihumbi makumi gabiri  
30.000 = Bihumbi makumi gashatu  
40.000 = Bihumbi makumi gana  
50.000 = Bihumbi makumi gatanu  
60.000 = Bihumbi makumi galindatu  
70.000 = Bihumbi makumi galinda  
80.000 = Bihumbi makumi galimunaana  
90.000 = Bihumbi makumi galimweenda  
100.000 = Bihumbi igana

## Kusha ku bihumbi igana kuhika ku bikumbi majana galimweenda (100.000-900.000)

100.000 = Bihumbi igana  
200.000 = Bihumbi magana gabiri  
300.000 = Bihumbi magana gashatu  
400.000 = Bihumbi magana gana  
500.000 = Bihumbi magana gatanu  
600.000 = Bihumbi magana galindatu  
700.000 = Bihumbi magana galinda  
800.000 = Bihumbi magana galimunaana  
900.000 = Bihumbi magana galimweenda

Ikyanya kera waharura ukuhisa ku bihumbi magana galimwenda, unahise na ku bihumbi magana galimwendwa na makumi galimweenda na mweenda na magana galimweenda na makumi galimweenda na mweenda **(999.999)**, hanakulikirage umuharuro tumuberangira **Umulyoni (1.000.000)**.

## Umulyoni (1.000.000)

Ukuharura ikyanya ukola ku mulyoni, kukolaga kuharura mu kuyubakira ku bilya kera twasoma: [Zero kandi li ubusha kandi li namuteneka ukuhisa ku ikumi](./zero-ku-ikumi), [Ikumi ku igana](./ikumi-ku-igana), [Igana ukuhisa ku kihumbi](./igana-ku-kihumbi).

Ikyanya kera waharura ukushagira ku mulyoni unahise ku milyoni majana galimweenda na makumi galimweenda na na mweenda ne'bihumbi magana galimweenda na makumi galimweenda na mwenda na maganja galimweenda na makumi galimweenda na na mweenda **(999.999.999)**, hanakulikirage **Umulyari (1.000.000.0000)**.
